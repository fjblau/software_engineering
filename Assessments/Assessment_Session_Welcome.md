## Important Things to Know

### This Is NOT a Pass/Fail Situation

- The assessment helps us understand your starting point
- Complete beginners are welcome and expected
- We're looking for curiosity and motivation, not existing skills
- There are no trick questions

### What We're Looking For

We're not looking for people who already know how to code. We're looking for:

- **Curiosity** — A genuine interest in learning how technology works
- **Persistence** — Willingness to work through challenges
- **Commitment** — Ability to dedicate time and energy to intensive learning
- **Collaboration** — Openness to working with others and receiving feedback

### What You Should Be Looking For

This is also your opportunity to evaluate us. Consider:

- Does the teaching style sound right for you?
- Is the time commitment realistic for your situation?
- Do the course outcomes align with your goals?
- Do you feel comfortable with the team and environment?

We teach **JavaScript and Python side-by-side** from day one. Why? Because professional developers understand programming concepts, not just one language's syntax. This approach makes you more versatile and adaptable.

You'll build a **production-ready capstone project** demonstrating:
- Full-stack web application
- Multiple programming languages
- Professional development practices
- Real-world problem solving

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need any prior programming experience?**
> No. Our course is designed for complete beginners. If you can use a computer comfortably and are willing to learn, you're ready.

**Q: What kind of computer do I need?**
> Any modern laptop (macOS, Windows, or Linux) with at least 8GB RAM. All software we use is free and open-source.

**Q: How much time outside of class should I expect to spend?**
> Plan for 5-10 hours per week for homework, practice, and review. The more you put in, the more you'll get out.

**Q: What can I do with these skills after the course?**
> Graduates are prepared for junior developer roles, freelance work, startup projects, or further specialized study. Our curriculum aligns with industry expectations for entry-level positions.

**Q: What if I fall behind?**
> We provide support through office hours, peer collaboration, and additional resources. The key is to communicate early if you're struggling.
